DOWNTOWN DAVIS, CA
The Happiest Store in Davis.
We are a thrift store located in the heart of Downtown Davis and have been serving our community with affordable and sustainable clothing since 2012. We are dedicated to providing unique and stylish clothing at affordable prices. Stop by and come visit our store today to browse our hand picked collection.
OUR STORY
Sustainable styles at unbeatable prices
In 2012, Boheme's original founder, Dawn
Donahue realized that there were no sustainable clothing options in Davis.
Being the change she wanted to see, she
started Boheme Thrift Store.
Yelena Ivashchenko, a local artisan and a
big believer in repurposing and recycling
"life leftovers" resonated with Donahue's
mission and took over the business in 2015
and has run it since.
